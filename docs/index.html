How It Works

CarrotSummary runs quietly in your menu bar and polls your active window every few seconds. It classifies your activity into categories like "Email", "Document Editing", "Development", etc. and tracks time per task.

The Pomodoro timer runs automatically ‚Äî 25 minutes of focus, then a 5-minute break, with a longer break every 4 sessions. When you switch between different types of work, CarrotSummary detects the context change and manages your sessions.
